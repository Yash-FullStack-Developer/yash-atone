Animal is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action drama film co-written, directed and edited by Sandeep Reddy
Vanga and produced by T-Series Films, Bhadrakali Pictures and Cine1 Studios. The film stars Ranbir Kapoor,
Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Rashmika Mandanna and Triptii Dimri.
